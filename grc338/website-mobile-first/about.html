About Us

Sweet Pea's Cafe serves amazing crepes, omelets, waffles, burgers, sandwiches, salads, soups, and more. Everything is made to order from the freshest local ingredients availble. Try our strawberry nutella crepe made with fresh strawberries grown in Watsonville, or our mixed berry and custard crepe with local berries and our gluten free custard. At Sweet Pea's Cafe our motto has always been It's All Good.

We offer deliver and Catering. Check us out before your next big event.
